Mission Statement

To provide community broadband access and facilitate the establishment of a virtual library system in Africa

Access to the Internet is an essential infrastructure for any community that cares about economic development, quality of life, and educational opportunities.

Technology for Humanity’s approach is to provide assistance to communities to build their own networks, using a variety of approaches. From municipal ownership to cooperatives to other nonprofit approaches.
